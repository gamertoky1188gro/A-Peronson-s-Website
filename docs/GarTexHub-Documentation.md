# GarTexHub Documentation Summary

Date: 2026-03-23

## Overview
GarTexHub is a B2B marketplace connecting global buyers with Bangladeshi factories and buying houses. The platform emphasizes verified communication, structured buyer requests, contract-based workflows, and auditable records.

## Account Types
- Buyer: Posts requests, receives quotes, verifies suppliers.
- Factory: Responds to requests, manages products and videos.
- Buying House: Manages multiple factory connections and staff IDs.

## Core Workflows
- Buyer Request Wizard: Garments vs Textile step-by-step form, custom fields, tech-pack upload, and verified-only messaging options.
- Messaging: Verified users message directly; unverified messages stay as requests.
- Verification: Subscription-based (first month $1.99, then $6.99). Restricted wallet credit applies.
- Contracts: Digital signatures, PDF contract vault, payment proof submission for Bank Transfer or LC.
- Calls: In-chat audio/video calls with recording for audit trails.

## Wallet, Coupons, and Early Adopters
- New accounts receive $5 restricted credit (verification/subscription only).
- Coupons support marketing attribution and restricted credit.
- Early-adopter coupons can grant free verification months (payment method required).

## Ratings
- Ratings are requested after milestones; if no user rating is submitted, the system auto-rates using signals or defaults to 5 stars.

## Notifications
- LinkedIn-style notification cards, smart search alerts, and monthly summaries.

## Analytics
- Buyer/Buying House metrics and marketplace owner KPIs: match rate, conversion rate, response time, demand trends, trust score.

## Support & Reports
- Dedicated Support/Feedback/Report page with category, priority, attachments, and tracking IDs.

## Moderation & Safety
- Strict third-party contact ban, spam/irrelevant content filtering, and staged penalties.

## UI & UX
- Professional LinkedIn-style layout, role-based pricing views, verified badges, and no fake demo data.
